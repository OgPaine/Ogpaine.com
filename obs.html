Useful Plug-ins and Scripts

Plugins

StreamFX-https://obsproject.com/forum/resources/streamfx-for-obs®-studio.578/

Adds a ton of features such as shader, sharpen, blur, colour chart, denouncing, upscaling and sdf effects. SDF effects are useful for making dropshadows.

Move transition-https://obsproject.com/forum/threads/move-transition.117568/

A really awesome plugin that enables you to move sources around your scenes without scene transitions. It is difficult to use at first.

win-capture-audio-https://obsproject.com/forum/threads/win-capture-audio.147240/

Makes it possible to capture audio sources separately. Desktop sounds are a thing of the past in streams

Scripts

Advanced Timer-https://obsproject.com/forum/resources/advanced-timer.637/

Easily configurable timer with a text source.

obs-filter-hotkeys-https://obsproject.com/forum/threads/obs-filter-hotkeys.132411/

The script allows you to set up keyboard shortcuts to change filters to obs.

Default scene on startup-https://obsproject.com/forum/threads/default-scene-on-startup.131742/

Obs can be opened to any scene as a default.
